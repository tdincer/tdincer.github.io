I participated in the Mechanism of Action (MoA) Prediction Challenge on Kaggle a month ago. The task was, given the cell viability and gene expression features for ~20,000 compounds, to predict the MoAs of the compounds. Each compound could be associated with more than 1 MoA (label), so it was a multilabel classification problem.

It was clear from the beginning of the competition that the way to go was going to be a blend of ensemble of models given the relatively small data size. So I stuck with that strategy and learned how to optimize the blending weights. Here I will show how to optimize the blending weights with the method of Langrange multipliers.
