I was a teaching assistant for the Inside The Milky Way course thought by Unal Ertan. 

The course content included:

- The structures of the Milky Way starting from the solar system: Birth of the modern science Galileo - Copernican revolution.
- Earth and the planets.
- Stars and star clusters.
- How does a star shine? - Electromagnetic spectrum, blackbody radiation, spectral lines, Doppler shift.
- Stellar structure and evolution - HR diagram.
- End points of stellar evolution: stellar explosions - supernova - white dwarfs, neutron stars and black holes.
- Satellite observatories and space research.
